# Image Inverter

**Image Inverter** is a [web application](https://image-inverter-five.vercel.app) designed to simplify bulk film scanning. It allows you to invert images quickly and efficiently from anywhere with an internet connection.

## ✅Features

- **Bulk Image Inverting** – Process multiple images at once, saving time and effort.
- **Accessible Anywhere** – Works entirely in your browser, no installations required.
- **Lightweight and Fast** – Optimized for speed without sacrificing performance.
- **Dark and light themes** - No need to strain your eyes while inverting the images.

## 🔐Privacy

All image processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring your files remain private. No images are uploaded to any server, so you can invert your photos with complete peace of mind.

## ❓How to Use

1. Open the [Image Inverter](https://image-inverter-five.vercel.app) web app.
2. Upload your images or drag and drop them into the browser.
3. Click "Invert" and download the results once ready.

## 🖼️Supported Formats

- JPEG
- PNG
- TIFF
- Other common image formats
